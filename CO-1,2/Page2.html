DERPATMENT NAME:CSE

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a field that focuses on the design, development, and use of computer systems and software. It covers topics like programming, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, computer networks, and cybersecurity. CSE students learn how to solve real-world problems using computers and technology, and they can work in areas such as software development, data science, robotics, and IT.
